13.12.2020:The Beginning of Fooday Restaurants

At Big Bite, we believe that great food brings people together. Our journey began in 2020 with a simple vision: to create a dining experience that combines exceptional cuisine with warm hospitality. We started as a small family-owned establishment, focusing on quality ingredients and traditional recipes passed down through generations.

Our founders, Alisha and Pasha Big Bite, poured their hearts into every dish, believing that food should not only nourish the body but also feed the soul. They created a menu that celebrates both local flavors and international influences, establishing the foundation for what would become one of the city's most beloved dining destinations.

10.12.2021:Expanding Our Horizons

By 2021, our reputation for quality and consistency had grown, allowing us to expand our operations. We introduced new menu items while maintaining our commitment to using fresh, locally-sourced ingredients. This period marked our first major renovation, transforming our cozy restaurant into a more spacious and inviting dining space.

We also began hosting special events and culinary workshops, sharing our passion for food with the community. These initiatives helped us build stronger relationships with our customers and established Big Bite as not just a restaurant, but a culinary hub where food lovers could gather, learn, and celebrate together.

16.10.2022:Award-Winning Recognition

The year 2022 brought significant recognition for our culinary excellence. We received our first "Best Fine Dining" award from the City Culinary Association, validating our commitment to quality and innovation. This achievement motivated us to further refine our techniques and expand our culinary team with talented chefs from diverse backgrounds.

During this period, we introduced our seasonal menu concept, allowing us to showcase the freshest ingredients available throughout the year. This approach not only enhanced the dining experience but also supported local farmers and producers, strengthening our connection to the community and reinforcing our sustainability values.

10.12.2023:Modernization and Growth

In 2025, we embraced technological advancements while preserving our traditional values. We launched our first website and online reservation system, making it easier for customers to book their dining experiences. We also expanded our catering services, bringing the Big Bite experience to weddings, corporate events, and private celebrations.

This era also saw the introduction of our chef's table experience, offering guests an intimate view of our kitchen operations and the opportunity to enjoy specially curated menus. These innovations helped us reach new audiences while maintaining the quality and personal touch that had become our signature.

13.12.2024:Culinary Innovation

By 2025, Big Bite had established itself as a leader in culinary innovation. We introduced molecular gastronomy techniques alongside traditional cooking methods, creating unique flavor combinations and presentations that delighted our guests. Our research and development team worked tirelessly to push the boundaries of what was possible in our kitchen.

We also launched our farm-to-table initiative, partnering with local organic farms to ensure the highest quality ingredients while reducing our environmental footprint. This commitment to sustainability and quality earned us recognition in several national food publications and solidified our reputation as a forward-thinking establishment.

13.12.2025:Looking to the Future

In 2025, we celebrated our 1th anniversary with a renewed commitment to excellence. We introduced our loyalty program, rewarding our regular customers with exclusive benefits and personalized dining experiences. We also expanded our team with world-class sommeliers and mixologists, enhancing our beverage program to complement our culinary offerings.

Looking ahead, we remain dedicated to innovation while honoring the traditions that made us successful. We continue to explore new culinary trends, invest in our team's development, and seek ways to enhance every aspect of the Big Bite experience. Our journey continues, fueled by our passion for exceptional food and genuine hospitality.
